Gulab Jamun

Ingredients

  • milk powder
  • rosewater
  • butter
  • baking powder
  • cardomom pods

Recipe

  1. Prepare and let the dough rest.
  2. Prepare the sugar syrup.
  3. Roll the Jamuns and fry it in boling Oil
  4. Add jamun in the Sugar syrup once the jamuns are fried.
  5. Garnish it with nuts or rose petals and serve.

Kaju katli

Ingredients

  • Cashews
  • Sugar
  • Butter
  • Ghee
  • water

Recipe

  1. Grind Cashews
  2. Prepare the sugar and cashew powder mixture.
  3. Knead the kaju katli mixture perfectly to a smooth dough.
  4. Take and greese the pan with Ghee.
  5. Put it on pan and use rolling pin to flatten it.
